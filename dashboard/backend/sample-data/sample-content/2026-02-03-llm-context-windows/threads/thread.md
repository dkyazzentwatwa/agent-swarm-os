### Post 2/12
First, what IS a context window?

Think of it like a desk. The bigger the desk, the more documents you can work with at once. AI models went from a school desk (4k tokens) to a conference table (200k) in 2 years.

### Post 3/12
The assumption: bigger desk = better work.

The reality: having a bigger desk doesn't make you a better reader.

Most developers dump everything into the context and hope for the best. That's like covering your conference table with every paper you own.

### Post 4/12
The real bottleneck? Attention.

Even models with 128k+ context use less than 10% of their context effectively for most tasks.
